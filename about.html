THE PROJECT

HOLO BARISTA is an immersive VR/XR experience that lets you become a virtual barista. Grab coffee cups, throw them around, and discover the joy of serving virtual coffee in augmented reality!

TECHNOLOGY

Built with cutting-edge web technologies: A-Frame for VR rendering, WebXR for immersive AR experiences, and physics simulation for realistic interactions. Works on Quest, WebXR-compatible browsers, and more!
